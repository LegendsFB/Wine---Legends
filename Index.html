Wine for Dummies

Simple wine knowledge without the snobbery.

🍷 Start Here

Wine doesn't have to be complicated. You don’t need to “detect saddle leather at sunset.” You just need to know what you like and why.

🍇 Red vs White

  • Red Wine – Made with grape skins. More tannin. Usually bigger structure.
  • White Wine – No skins during fermentation. Higher acidity. Fresher profile.

🌍 Common Grapes

  • Pinot Grigio – Light, crisp, easy.
  • Chardonnay – From clean and citrusy to rich and buttery.
  • Pinot Noir – Elegant, red fruit, low tannin.
  • Cabernet Sauvignon – Bold, structured, dark fruit.

🥂 How to Order Wine

Skip the vague stuff. Instead of saying “I want something smooth,” try:

  • “I like high acid wines.”
  • “I prefer low tannin reds.”
  • “I want something dry and mineral.”
